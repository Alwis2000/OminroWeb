About Us

Our company's start originates from 2006 when Ominro Medicals was incorporated as a partnership. In 2008, Ominro Medicals (Private) Limited was founded as a private limited liability company in the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

Our objective is to continue to tap into the ever growing Health Care and Food Industry markets with the intention of expanding their potential product portfolio.
