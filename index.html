About Us

Ominro Medicals Pvt Ltd was established and registered in the Democratic Republic of Sri Lanka in 2008. Our core philosophy revolves around tapping into the ever-expanding Medical, Health Care, and Non-Medical industries. We aim to expand our product and service offerings by providing valuable information to our current and prospective principals, helping them diversify their product portfolios.

As we navigate an external environment that is changing at an unprecedented rate, we anticipate more transformation in the next three years than we have seen in the past. In 2011, we diversified into manufacturing, with a focus on the pharmaceutical industry. We have also become a leading importer and representative for international brands in the marketing and distribution of medical, healthcare, biomedical, non-medical, and GMP industrial products in Sri Lanka.

Our extensive product portfolio includes:
  • In-vitro diagnostics reagents test kits.
  • Biomedical equipment.
  • Accessories and consumables.
  • Disposables (including surgical).
  • Glassware and plasticware products.
  • GMP industrial disposables.

    • Reflecting on our progress and success, we are proud to have achieved the objectives and goals set by our principal suppliers and ourselves. This has established Ominro Medicals as a key player in the medical, healthcare, and non-medical industries in both the public and private sectors.

    Over the years, we have built strong relationships with leading government and private healthcare corporations, industrial manufacturers, and food processing organizations in Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to selecting and supplying products of the highest accepted standards. Our skilled professionals enable us to identify the needs of the medical profession and consumers, ensuring we upgrade technologies to improve product development in response to the environment.

    Our Nutraceuticals

    The Global and Sri Lankan community is in the process of a rapid change over to "Live Healthy with a Wise Lifestyle" is boosting the need for Nutraceuticals.

    The worldwide nutraceutical market is anticipated to be around 722.49 billon between 2021 - 2026 with an annual compound rate of 8.3 percent. As per a research reports the market size for pharmaceuticals and nutraceuticals in Sri Lanka was worth USD 642 million in 2018, which the experts are expecting to reach USD 787 million growing at the CAGR (compound annual growth rate) of 4.1 percent.

    We look forward to have manufacturers / exporters with quality "Nutraceuticals" products derived from food sources with extra health benefits in addition to the basic nutritional value found in foods. Nutraceuticals are considered to be healthy sources for the prevention of life-threatening diseases such as diabetes, renal and gastrointestinal disorders, as well as different infections.

    We are committed make Sri Lankan's "Live Healthy with a Wise Lifestyle" with your brand of Nutraceuticals.
